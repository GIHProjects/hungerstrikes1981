Bobby Sands, IRA

Born:

March 9, 1954 in Rathcoole, Belfast

Died:

May 5, 1981 at age 27

Conviction:

October 1977, 14 years for possession of firearms

Began Strike:

March 1

Days on Strike:

66

Candidacy:

April, Fermanagh-South Tyrone by-election, won by over 30,000 votes

Martin Hurson, IRA

Born:

September 14, 1956 in Aughnaskea, Dungannon

Died:

July 13, 1981 at age 25

November 1977, 20 years for possession of landmines and conspiracy, as well as two other sentences of 15 and 5 years respectively, the sentences to run concurrently

May 29

46
